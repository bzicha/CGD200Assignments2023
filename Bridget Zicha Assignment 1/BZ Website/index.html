My name is Bridget Zicha.

As a Canadian, the first place that I recommend that you visit is Montreal, Quebec. I grew up in this beautiful French speaking city and love that it is a piece of Europe within North America! The second place that I recommend that you visit is Neuchatel, Switzerland. I spent a year there and it will always be one of my favourite places. Its proximity to the lake and to the alps makes it a breathtaking area to visit. The third and final place that I recommend that you visit is Dublin, Ireland. On top of being one of the friendliest places in the world, Ireland has great lush and green landscape.

  • Visit Neuchatel!
